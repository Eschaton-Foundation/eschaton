
# Miscellaneous

This section contains important information about your license, available add-ons, and the plugin's version history.

---

### License

*   **Where to find your key:** Your license key is available in your purchase confirmation email and on your account page on the CWP Snippets website.
*   **How to activate:** Go to **Snippets > License**. Paste your key into the field and click "Activate License".
*   **License Status:** This page will show the current status of your license, including the expiration date and the number of activations used.

---

## Add-ons

Add-ons are pre-built collections of snippets that extend the functionality of CWP Snippets. They are designed to be imported directly into your plugin.

*   **Accessing Add-ons:** You can browse and download available add-ons from the CWP Snippets Add-ons library at `cwpsnippets.com`.
*   **Installing Add-ons:** Once you obtain an add-on, you import it into CWP Snippets using the **Snippets > Import/Export ( Pro )** feature. Add-ons are imported as new snippets (e.g., Snippets, Templates, Samples, Functions, Styles, Scripts) that become accessible within your CWP Snippets library for editing and customization.
*   **Managing Add-ons:** The **Snippets > Add-ons** page provides information about available add-ons and may offer management options for those you have installed.

---

### Version History (Changelog)

A detailed changelog for every version of CWP Snippets is maintained in the `readme.txt` file in the plugin's main directory. This file provides a complete record of new features, improvements, and bug fixes.

**To view the changelog:**

1.  You can open the `readme.txt` file directly from the plugin files.
2.  Alternatively, on the main **Plugins** page in your WordPress admin, click the "View details" link for CWP Snippets. This will open a modal window that includes the changelog.
